About Us

History

AMS was established in Bahrain in 1976, it started out as a small garage and then over the years it has built many trustworthy relationships with it's customers and recognized as an airplane maintainenance company thanks to the professional staff.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To find solutions to every possible airplane defects and fix them as soon as possible, this is essential because it minimizes the risk of an airplane crashing and ensures that travellers have a safe and a pleasent flight.

Vision: To be the best at coming up with airplane maintainenance solutions.
